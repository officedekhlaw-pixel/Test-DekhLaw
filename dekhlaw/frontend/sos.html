Emergency Scenarios

When to Use SOS

SOS is built for genuine legal emergencies. Use it when every minute matters.

Detained or Arrested

You or a family member has been detained and needs immediate legal advice on rights and bail.

Urgent Legal Notice

You received a notice, summons, or warrant requiring an immediate legal response.

At a Court Proceeding

You are at a hearing without representation and need urgent legal guidance right now.

Forced Entry / Eviction

You are facing an unlawful eviction, forced entry, or dispossession of property.
